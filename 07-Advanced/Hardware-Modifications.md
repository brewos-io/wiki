# Hardware Modifications

Advanced hardware topics and modification guides.

## ⚠️ Safety Warning

```
⚠️  WARNING: MAINS VOLTAGE

Hardware modifications involve 100-240V AC mains electricity.
Improper handling can result in death or serious injury.

• Only qualified individuals should perform modifications
• Always disconnect power before making changes
• Use isolation transformers during development
• Never work alone on energized equipment
• Follow all local electrical codes and regulations
```
